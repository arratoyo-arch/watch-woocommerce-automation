# Customer-Friendly Pages for watch-tokyo.com

This document collects low-risk, customer-friendly copy that can be manually added to watch-tokyo.com pages, menus, footer links, and Woo Draft Bridge (WDB) product draft templates.

The goal is to reduce purchase anxiety for overseas customers before they place an order. These texts are content proposals only: they do not create WordPress pages automatically, update published products in bulk, change prices, change stock, process orders, or change payment settings.

## Recommended Site Placement

* Home: add a “Why Buy from Watch Tokyo?” section.
* Footer: add links to About, Shipping, Customs & Import Duties, Returns & Cancellations, FAQ, and Contact.
* Product pages: include a shared trust block covering Condition, Shipping, Customs / Import Duties, Before Purchase, and Note.
* WDB drafts: keep products as drafts until a human confirms price, stock, images, category, specifications, and final description.

## 1. About Watch Tokyo

### Page Title

About Watch Tokyo

### Suggested Copy

Watch Tokyo offers authentic Japanese watches sourced from Japan.

We focus on Japanese domestic models / JDM watches from trusted brands such as Casio, Seiko, Citizen, and Orient.

All items are carefully checked before shipping and packed securely for international delivery.

Our goal is to make it easy for customers around the world to purchase Japanese watches with confidence.

If you have any questions about a model, size, specifications, shipping, or availability, please contact us before placing your order.

## 2. Shipping

### Page Title

Shipping

### Suggested Copy

We offer free international shipping from Japan.

All items are carefully packed and shipped with tracking whenever available.

Shipping times may vary depending on the destination country, customs processing, and local delivery conditions.

After your order is shipped, tracking information will be provided when available.

Please make sure your shipping address is correct before placing your order.

## 3. Customs & Import Duties

### Page Title

Customs & Import Duties

### Suggested Copy

Import duties, taxes, customs fees, brokerage fees, or other charges may be required by your country.

These charges are not included in the item price and are the buyer's responsibility where applicable.

Customs policies vary by country, so please check with your local customs office before purchasing if you are unsure.

We cannot control customs delays or additional charges after the item arrives in your country.

## 4. Returns & Cancellations

### Page Title

Returns & Cancellations

### Suggested Copy

Please contact us before returning any item.

Returns may be accepted only when the item is unused, unworn, and returned in the same condition as received.

The buyer may be responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is caused by our error.

Please check the model number, size, specifications, and compatibility carefully before purchasing.

If there is a problem with your order, please contact us as soon as possible so we can review the issue.

Yes. Watch Tokyo offers authentic Japanese watches sourced from Japan.

Items are new / unused unless otherwise stated on the product page. Please check each product description before purchase.

We ship from Japan.

## WDB Draft Template Connection Policy

Customer-friendly WDB product drafts should include the shared trust block without automatically publishing or bulk-updating existing public products.

Recommended WDB draft description structure:

1. Model
2. Brand
3. Series
4. Key Features
5. Condition
6. Shipping
7. Customs / Import Duties
8. Before Purchase
9. Note

Human review is required before publishing a WDB draft. The reviewer should confirm:

* Price
* Stock
* Images
* Category
* Tags
* Model number
* Specifications
* Size and compatibility
* Final English description

This keeps the workflow semi-automatic, human-reviewed, preview-before-production, and selected-rows-only.
