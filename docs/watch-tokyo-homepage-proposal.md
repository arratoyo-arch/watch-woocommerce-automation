# Watch Tokyo Homepage Proposal

## Goal

Make the home page immediately understandable for overseas customers who want authentic Japanese watches shipped from Japan.

## Proposed home page sections

### 1. Hero section

**Main headline**

Authentic Japanese Watches, Shipped Worldwide from Japan

**Sub headline**

Shop Japan domestic model watches from trusted Japanese brands, carefully sourced, packed, and shipped internationally with tracking.

**Primary CTA**

Shop JDM Watches

**Secondary CTA**

Shop by Brand

**Trust points under hero**

* Authentic products sourced from Japan
* Free international shipping from Japan
* Tracking and careful packing
* Questions welcome before ordering

### 2. Why buy from Watch Tokyo

Use a three- or four-column section:

1. **Sourced in Japan** — We focus on authentic Japanese watches and Japan domestic models.
2. **Clear product information** — Model number, condition, shipping, and buyer notes are shown before purchase.
3. **Careful international shipping** — Orders are packed carefully and shipped from Japan with tracking.
4. **Human-reviewed listings** — Product drafts are reviewed before publication for price, stock, images, and specifications.

### 3. Featured JDM watches

Show a small manually selected group of published products.

Recommended rules:

* Start with 4 to 8 products.
* Choose clean product photos and confirmed stock.
* Include popular Japanese models and easy-to-understand everyday watches.
* Do not show unreviewed drafts.

### 4. Shop by brand

Recommended brand cards:

* Casio
* Seiko
* Citizen
* Orient

Each card should link to a filtered brand archive or category page.

### 5. Shop by category

Recommended category cards:

* G-SHOCK
* Solar Watches
* Radio Controlled Watches
* Digital Watches
* Diver Style Watches
* Everyday Watches

### 6. New arrivals

Show recently published products after human review.

Recommended label:

New Arrivals from Japan

### 7. Shipping from Japan

Suggested copy:

We ship internationally from Japan with tracking. Eligible products include free international shipping unless otherwise stated on the product page. Each item is carefully packed for overseas delivery.

### 8. Customs / import duties note

Suggested copy:

Import duties, taxes, and customs fees may be charged by your country and are the buyer's responsibility where applicable. Please check your local rules before ordering.

### 9. FAQ preview

Recommended questions:

* Are the watches authentic?
* Are these Japan domestic models?
* Do you ship internationally?
* Is tracking included?
* Who pays customs or import duties?
* What should I check before purchase?

Link to the full FAQ page.

### 10. Contact

Suggested copy:

Not sure if a model is right for you? Please contact us before ordering. We are happy to help you check model number, specifications, size, and compatibility.

## Footer navigation recommendation

Add clear footer links:

* About Watch Tokyo
* Shipping Policy
* Customs / Import Duties
* Returns / Refunds
* FAQ
* Contact

This proposal should be implemented manually in WordPress or through a separate reviewed PR. This phase does not add top-page update automation.
